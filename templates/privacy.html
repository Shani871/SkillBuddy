Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2025

SkillBuddy (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform—via our website, mobile application, or other means.

1. Information We Collect
  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, academic details, parent/teacher contact details.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, IP address, device/browser type.
  • Emotional and Behavioral Data: Mood/emotion recognition, task productivity patterns.
  • Media Files: Face images (with consent), uploaded assignments/documents.
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To provide academic and emotional support
  • To track well-being and offer improvement tips
  • To send reminders and manage productivity
  • To enable communication between students, parents, and teachers
  • To improve services through analytics
3. Sharing Your Information

We never sell or rent your data. We may share data:

  • With your consent to parents/teachers
  • With backend service providers
  • If required by law
4. Your Rights
  • Access, update, or delete your data
  • Opt out of tracking features
  • Withdraw face/emotion recognition consent anytime

Contact us at support@skillbuddy.ai

5. Data Security

We use encryption, secure storage, and access control. But no system is 100% secure—please protect your credentials.

6. Children’s Privacy

SkillBuddy is for users above age 13. If you’re under 13, use the platform only with parental or guardian consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Major updates will be communicated via email or in-app.
